How to Enable JavaScript

Windows

  1. Open your preferred browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
  2. Navigate to the browser's settings or options menu.
  3. Find the "Privacy and Security" or "Content Settings" section.
  4. Locate the "JavaScript" option and ensure it's set to "Allowed" or "Enabled."
  5. Refresh this page to apply the changes.

Mac

  1. Launch your preferred browser (e.g., Safari, Chrome, Firefox).
  2. Access the browser's "Preferences" or "Settings" menu.
  3. Look for the "Security" or "Privacy" section.
  4. Find the "Enable JavaScript" option and ensure it's checked or enabled.
  5. Refresh this page to apply the changes.

If you need further assistance, please consult your browser's help documentation or search online for specific instructions tailored to your browser and operating system.
